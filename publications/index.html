Publications

2025

  1. arXiv
    Kinship Data Benchmark for Multi-hop Reasoning
    Tianda Sun and Dimitar Kazakov
    arXiv preprint arXiv:2601.07794, 2025
    Abs arXiv Bib Code

    We introduce KinshipQA, a contamination-proof benchmark evaluating LLM multi-hop reasoning across 7 culturally diverse kinship systems. We evaluate 6 state-of-the-art LLMs on 3,134 questions with controlled reasoning complexity (1-4 hops), revealing significant performance degradation as reasoning depth increases.

  2. RANLP
    KGEIR: Knowledge Graph-Enhanced Iterative Reasoning for Multi-Hop Question Answering
    Tianda Sun and Dimitar Kazakov
    In Proceedings of the RANLP 2025 Workshop on Robust and Low-Resource Large Language Models (R2LM), 2025
    Abs Bib Code

    We propose KGEIR, a framework combining LLM reasoning with dynamic knowledge graph construction. KGEIR achieves competitive and superior performance on HotpotQA, 2WikiMultiHopQA, and MuSiQue benchmarks compared to state-of-the-art RAG methods, with a 4.5% F1 improvement over Tree-of-Thought baseline.

    Tianda Sun, Jamie Carr, and Dimitar Kazakov
